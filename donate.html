Donation Portal

Thank you for supporting our mission.

All Funds are 100% used directly & indirectly for the benefit of rescued animals. Funds will be used for such things as: ordinary and extraordinary medical care, Heartworm testing/preventative/treatment, spay/neuter, microchips, vaccinations, flea treatments, food, treats, toys, beds, blankets, collars, leashes, cleaning supplies, transportation, administration, advertising, adoption events, fundraiser events, facility maintenance. We also support/fund other rescue organizations & animal non-profits.

*PayPal covers all transaction costs for donations made on this site, ensuring that 100% of each gift made is available to charity. Your donation will be made to PayPal Giving Fund, an IRS-registered 501(c)(3) public charity (Federal Tax ID: 45-0931286), and we will provide the receipt for your donation. We verify that receiving charities have tax-exempt status with the IRS and are not on recognized economic sanctions lists. Charities enrolled with PayPal Giving Fund will typically receive funds in their PayPal accounts within 15 to 45 days after PayPal Giving Fund’s receipt of a donation. Charities not enrolled with PayPal Giving Fund will be additionally vetted and if eligible, will receive funds via other means, such as a check, typically within 90 days. PayPal Giving Fund holds all donations in a non-interest bearing account. PayPal Giving Fund retains ultimate discretion over all donations it receives and, in limited cases, may be required to redirect funds when a charity does not meet legal or other eligibility requirements. for more information, please see PayPal Giving Fund's Nonprofit Certification Policy and Donation Delivery Policy.
